1. Service Description & Objectives

Service Name: Data Center Fabric Health & Optimization Review (Fixed-Price Engagement)

Purpose: To provide a vendor-agnostic, deep-dive assessment of the client's production Data Center fabric health, stability, and policy configuration. The objective is to identify critical risk factors, policy debt, and optimization opportunities.

Applicable Technologies:

  • Cisco ACI (APIC, Leaf/Spine fabric)
  • Cisco Nexus (NX-OS, VXLAN/EVPN fabrics)
  • Juniper Networks (Junos-based DC fabrics)

2. Scope of Work & Deliverables

This fixed-price engagement is a 5-day, observation-only analysis resulting in a comprehensive written report and a presentation.

# Deliverable Name Description
D1 Data Collection Summary Verification of read-only access and a summary of configuration files and state data reviewed.
D2 Detailed Findings Report A formal PDF document (max. 25 pages) containing: Critical Risk Factors, Policy Debt Analysis, and Optimization Recommendations.
D3 Health Score Audit Specific metrics and health scores from the controller/CLI, with trend analysis.
D4 Executive Review Session One (1) 60-minute remote session to present the findings and answer clarifying questions.
